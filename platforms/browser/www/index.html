    Application for Real Time Bus Arrival Predictions

    This application works for any bus stop within the area of London.

    To predict the bus arrival time is a quick two step process. You have to enter the bus route number (196 or N35 etc.) and select the desired bus stop from the list. All bus arrival time predictions are in real time, gathered from TFL official open data stream.

    The bus arrival times are automatically updated every 15 seconds.

    The last search is saved in phone memory and will be popping up next time when the app will be used.

    For new search press the "New Search" from the main page.
